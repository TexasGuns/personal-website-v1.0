Sample 1 — Leaf Landscape Supply

Operations & Business Intern

OverviewOver the course of a 10-week immersive internship, I gained hands-on experience at one of Austin's premier re-wholesaling nurseries. Operating in a fast-paced environment supporting both landscape contractors and the general public, I developed a comprehensive understanding of how large-scale inventory management, sales, and cross-functional operations drive business growth in the green industry.

Key Contributions & Projects
  • Cross-Functional Operations: Rotated across all major departments—including sales, inventory, receiving, deliveries, accounting, and HR—to gain a top-down view of nursery operations and supply chain management.
  • B2B Sales & Client Relations: Shadowed the senior B2B sales team, mastering the internal sales system to build customer quotes and manage client accounts. Completed targeted B2B sales training.
  • AI Chatbot Integration: Spearheaded a unique technical project for the HR department by setting up an AI-powered chatbot fed with internal documents for employee rapid training.
  • Inventory & Logistics: Managed physical plant inventory, checked in incoming shipments, and verified the accuracy of outbound deliveries.
Skills & Takeaways
  • Industry Knowledge: Rapidly learned on-the-fly plant identification, complex project sourcing, and nursery profit margin analysis. Attended the TNLA Nursery/Landscape Expo in San Antonio.
  • Communication & Problem Solving: Overcame communication barriers with a diverse customer base by practicing patient, active listening to accurately fulfill challenging requirements.
  • Business Acumen: Successfully bridged my Horticulture major and Business minor, seeing firsthand how efficient inventory management directly maximizes cash flow and revenue generation.

Sample 2 — GreenLeaf Nursery

Commercial Nursery Production Intern

OverviewOver the course of an 8-week rotational internship at GreenLeaf Nursery Company, I gained a comprehensive view of large-scale commercial nursery operations. Operating at one of the top producing nurseries in the country, I worked directly alongside supervisors and labor crews to master the lifecycle of producing high-quality trees and shrubs for the B2B green industry.

Key Contributions & Projects
  • Nursery Operations & Production: Gained extensive hands-on experience by completing multi-day rotations with every major supervisor and labor crew. Executed essential tasks including shifting plants, shearing, removing diseased branches, and managing dead plant disposal.
  • Disaster Recovery: Played a critical role in facility recovery efforts following Hurricane Beryl by removing damaged shade cloth and plastic from overwintering structures and salvaging inventory to minimize crop loss.
  • Operational Analysis: Analyzed workforce management and labor efficiency, observing the complex challenges of seasonal labor hours and considering operational models utilizing H2A programs to stabilize workforce consistency.
Skills & Takeaways
  • Horticultural Expertise: Developed practical, technical field skills including proper plant shearing protocols, soil sampling, integrated pest identification, and critical assessment of plant sale-readiness.
  • Resilience & Adaptability: Overcame extreme climate conditions and significant language barriers by actively learning conversational Spanish to effectively communicate commands and collaborate closely with labor crews.
