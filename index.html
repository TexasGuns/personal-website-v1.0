About Me

As a third-generation green industry professional and Texas A&M Horticulture graduate, I have dedicated my academic and professional life to the Texas green industry. I started working on my family's shade tree farm at 15 and have since gained direct experience in nursery production, rewholesale distribution, and landscape installation. I bring a 360-degree understanding of the industry.

Now based in the DFW area, I am pursuing a sales or management role at a rewholesale nursery or landscape company.
